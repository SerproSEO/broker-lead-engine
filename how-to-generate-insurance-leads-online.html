    • Use Google Posts to highlight special offers and seasonal campaigns

  2. High-Converting Landing Pages

    Pro Tip: Create separate landing pages for each insurance type and traffic source. A generic page converts at 2-4%, while targeted pages convert at 8-15%.

7 Costly Mistakes That Kill Lead Generation ROI

After analyzing hundreds of insurance agency marketing campaigns, these are the most common mistakes that waste budgets and reduce lead quality:

  1. Sending All Traffic to Your Homepage

    The Problem: Generic homepages convert at 1-3%, while dedicated landing pages convert at 8-15%.

    The Solution: Create specific landing pages for each traffic source and insurance type.

    Quick Fix: Start with one landing page for your most popular insurance type and A/B test different versions.

  2. Not Tracking Phone Calls

    The Problem: 70% of insurance leads call rather than fill out forms, but most agencies don't track which marketing channels drive phone calls.

    The Solution: Use call tracking numbers for each marketing channel.

    Quick Fix: Get a Google forwarding number for your Google Ads campaigns to start tracking call conversions.

  3. Slow Response Times

    The Problem: Leads contacted within 5 minutes are 9x more likely to convert than those contacted after 30 minutes.

    The Solution: Set up instant notifications and have a process to respond within 5 minutes.

    Quick Fix: Use your CRM's mobile app to get instant lead notifications and call from your car if necessary.
