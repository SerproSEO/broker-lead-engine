1. Search Engine Optimization (SEO) - Your 24/7 Sales Machine

SEO is the foundation of insurance digital marketing. Rank #1 for "insurance agent [your city]" and watch qualified leads pour in automatically.

The Insurance SEO Blueprint

Local SEO Domination (Most Important)
  • Optimize Google My Business with 50+ photos, posts weekly, Q&A section
  • Build 100+ local citations (Yelp, YellowPages, local directories)
  • Create location pages for every city within 25 miles
  • Get 5+ reviews monthly with specific keyword mentions
  • Implement local schema markup on all pages
Content Strategy That Ranks
  • Target "[insurance type] + [city]" keywords (500+ searches/month)
  • Create ultimate guides for each insurance type (3,000+ words)
  • Answer "People Also Ask" questions in dedicated sections
  • Update content quarterly with fresh statistics and examples
  • Internal link to related content and service pages
Technical SEO Excellence
  • Page speed under 3 seconds (lose 40% of visitors at 3+ seconds)
  • Mobile-first responsive design (67% search on mobile)
  • SSL certificate and HTTPS (required for rankings)
  • XML sitemap submitted to Google Search Console
  • Fix all 404 errors and broken links monthly

  • 300% increase in organic traffic within 6 months
